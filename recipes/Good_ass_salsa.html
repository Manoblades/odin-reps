Good Ass Salsa

Description

I made this salsa one day out of the blue because I was bored and wanted to try to make salsa from scratch. I soon learned that "from scratch" is kinda hard so I cheated a bit but it's still really good.

Ingredients
  • 4 cans of Roasted Garlic Diced Tomatoes.
  • 2 cans of Contadina Tomato Paste.
  • 2 big purple onions.
  • 2 bundles of cilantro.
  • 2 packs of pico de gallo medium heat.
  • 2 packs of pico de gallo spicy.
  • 5 fresh jalapenos.
  • 5 serrano peppers.
  • Garlic salt.
Steps
  1. Pour 2 cans of diced tomatoes into a bowl.
  2. Dice the onions and cilantro and add them into the bowl.
  3. Add 2 packs of pico de gallo, one medium, one spicy.
  4. Add garlic salt and mix well.
  5. In a blender, add one pack of pico de gallo and both cans of tomato paste.
  6. Add diced peppers to blender as well with garlic salt.
  7. Blend.
  8. Pour the mix into the bowl with the chopped salsa.
  9. Stir everything and let it rest.
  10. Eat it.
