Spicy Pasta

Description

This recipe is not for the faint of heart. I mean it. This shit is spicy as fuck and it includes product placement so get out your fucking checkbooks and pay me for this shit if your brand name is mentioned here.

Ingredients
  • Your pasta of choice. For this one we will be using Quattrotini.
  • An alfredo sauce base.
  • A can of diced tomatoes that are seasoned with roasted garlic.
  • Carolina Reaper cheese from Walmart.
  • Raw jalapenos and serrano peppers.
