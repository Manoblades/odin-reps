Spicy Pasta

Description

This recipe is not for the faint of heart. I mean it. This shit is spicy as fuck and it includes product placement so get out your fucking checkbooks and pay me for this shit if your brand name is mentioned here.

Ingredients
  • Your pasta of choice. For this one we will be using Quattrotini.
  • An alfredo sauce base.
  • A can of diced tomatoes that are seasoned with roasted garlic.
  • Carolina Reaper cheese from Walmart.
  • Raw jalapenos and serrano peppers.
  • Red onions and white onions.
  • Minced garlic.
  • "I can't feel my face" from Flatiron Red Pepper Company.
  • Onion Powder
  • Garlic Powder
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Sugar (for taming acidity)
Steps
  1. Put two pots on the stove. One with water and one with nothing.
  2. Start heating the pot with water in it.
  3. Salt the water and pour a little bit of olive oil in it.
  4. Pour your pasta into the water.
  5. In the other pot, pour a small amount of olive oil in the bottom.
  6. Throw in diced onions and peppers, both kinds of each.
  7. Toss in some minced garlic as well.
  8. Wait for the onions and peppers to start getting brown.
  9. Once they're brown, pour in the alfredo sauce base and the diced tomatoes.
  10. Add in all of the seasonings and the cheese.
  11. Add a lot of the "I can't feel my face" pepper flakes. Really drench it.
  12. Stir and taste repeatedly to see if more seasoning is needed.
  13. Use sugar to cut down on acidity if needed.
  14. Drain pasta once cooked.
  15. Keep sauce on low and serve pasta and sauce together.
